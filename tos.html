Terms of Service

Store Bot terms of service.

These terms explain acceptable use, payment handling, Premium features, AutoPay forwarding, and support for Store Bot.

Use of Store Bot

Store Bot is provided as a Discord storefront and order management tool for digital products, services, and community perks. Server owners are responsible for what they sell and for following Discord rules, payment provider rules, and applicable laws.

Payments and refunds

Store Bot is not a payment processor and does not directly hold customer funds. Payments are handled outside the bot through the payment methods configured by each server owner. Refund policies are controlled by the seller unless separately stated by SMOKNetworks.

Premium access

Premium features may include more product slots, forwarding AutoPay, discounts, gift cards, custom branding, staff roles, and logs. Premium access can be changed, revoked, or limited if the bot is abused or used for prohibited activity.

AutoPay forwarding

Forwarding AutoPay is designed to match payment notification emails to pending Store Bot orders. It is not a guarantee that every payment email will be detected, parsed, or matched. Server owners should still monitor their own payment accounts.

Prohibited use

Do not use Store Bot to sell illegal goods, stolen goods, regulated weapons, drugs, adult sexual services, gambling products, malware, phishing tools, or anything that violates Discord policies or payment provider rules.
