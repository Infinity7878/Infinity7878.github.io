Privacy Policy

Store Bot privacy policy.

Last updated: June 2026. This page explains what Store Bot may store to operate Discord storefronts, orders, staff tools, logs, and AutoPay features.

Data Store Bot may store

  • Discord server IDs, channel IDs, role IDs, user IDs, and order IDs.
  • Store settings such as store title, support channel, staff roles, logs channel, product settings, prices, discounts, gift cards, and delivery text.
  • Order data such as product selected, order code, order status, amount, currency, timestamps, and delivery status.
  • Premium status and plan-related configuration.

AutoPay forwarding data

Premium servers can use AutoPay forwarding by forwarding PayPal or Cash App payment notification emails to a Store Bot forwarding address. Store Bot may process forwarded email data such as provider, amount, currency, order code, payment status, forwarding alias, sender metadata, and message timestamp so it can match a pending order.

Server owners should not forward their full inbox. Store Bot only needs payment notification emails that relate to Store Bot orders.

How data is used

  • To create and manage Discord store panels.
  • To track orders and delivery status.
  • To notify staff through logs.
  • To match payment notifications to pending orders.
  • To provide premium features such as AutoPay, discounts, gift cards, staff roles, and custom branding.

Data deletion

Users can request deletion of their stored user data through Store Bot privacy commands or by contacting the support server. Server-level store and order data may need to remain available to server owners for order history, support, abuse prevention, and accounting-style records.

Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, join the support server: discord.gg/zB7NgPBzBA.
