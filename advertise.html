FAQ

Common questions

Who sees my ad?

Sponsor cards appear for free Store Bot users across the placements you select. Premium subscribers do not see ads.

What can I promote?

Discord-relevant services, tools, communities, and stores—especially offers that help sellers operate or grow. Unrelated, misleading, or prohibited content is rejected.

When does my campaign start?

The purchased duration begins after manual approval, not immediately at payment. You will be contacted if your ad needs changes.

Can I choose specific placements?

Yes. Pick one or more of the four surfaces during checkout. You can run the same copy everywhere or narrow to the surfaces that fit your offer best.

What if checkout is unavailable?

If Stripe checkout is temporarily disabled, contact support and we can help arrange a sponsor slot manually.

Can I edit my ad after launch?

Contact support with your campaign details if you need copy or URL changes during an active run.
