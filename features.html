Features

Discord store bot features for digital sellers.

Store Bot gives server owners the tools to sell products and services directly inside Discord while keeping orders organized for staff.

🛒

Discord store panels

Post a clean product panel where customers can pick items, view prices, and begin checkout directly in Discord.

📦

Digital product sales

Use Store Bot to sell VIP access, license keys, serial keys, downloads, configs, setup packages, and digital services.

✅

Order tracking

Track pending, paid, delivered, cancelled, and completed orders with Discord-friendly tools for staff.

💸

Forwarding AutoPay

Premium servers can forward PayPal or Cash App payment notifications so Store Bot can match order codes and amounts.

🏷️

Discount codes

Create promotions, sales codes, and customer incentives for your Discord storefront.

🎁

Gift cards

Give customers store credit that can be redeemed toward future orders.

🛡️

Staff roles and logs

Let trusted staff manage products and orders while logging store activity to a private channel.

🤖

Bot hosting available

Discord bot hosting is available separately through SMOKNetworks for customers who need their own bot kept online.
