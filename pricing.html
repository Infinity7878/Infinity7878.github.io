Which plan should I use?

Use Free to test the panel, products, and order flow. Use Premium when you are actively selling and want more product slots, cleaner branding, AutoPay forwarding, discounts, gift cards, staff roles, and logs. Premium is $3/month per server.

How do I upgrade?

Click Buy Premium, log in with Discord, choose the server you want to upgrade, and complete checkout through Stripe. Premium is applied automatically to the selected server after the Stripe webhook confirms payment.
